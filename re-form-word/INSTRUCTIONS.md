# Re-Form Word Challenge

Take a word that has been into 2 parts. Re-form the word by adding both halves together, changing the first character to an uppercase letter.

Difficulty: Very Easy

## Examples

* "seas" + "onal" = "Seasonal"
* "comp" + "lete" = "Complete"
* "lang" + "uage" = "Language"
